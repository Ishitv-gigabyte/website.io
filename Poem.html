You know I enjoy reading poetry but I have never written one. I hope this first(and probably a terrible) attempt at poetry might convince you:

Hides behind her doe eyes
Little does she reveal about her own cries
Drowned in her own pain
Doesn't want her struggle to go in vain

Heart of an angel, beauty of an enchantress
In her presence, life feels like a sweet caress
She is too fair, too wise; wisely too fair
To merit bliss by making me despair

Promises to first teachers being her guiding light
Little does she see through her own sight
A glimpse of her tenderheart is all I want
Seeds of trust I want to plant

Withered by my own emotions, I knock her door
Reminded of the promises to first teachers
But as I please and implore, she shuts the door
Leaving me alone, in the night's chill

I question the mirror
Am I really someone
Who her guiding light won't approve of?
Mirror responds
'You are the white knight, beacon of light
winning through each daunting fight'

I pray to the gods
Give her wisdom to realise
Her teachers are beware of dark knights
Her teachers will entrust the 'White Knight'
